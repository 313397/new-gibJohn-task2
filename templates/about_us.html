About Us

We are GibJohn Tutoring and we are a tutoring company that provides face-to-face sessions for learners as well as allowing learners to monitor their own progress, access resources and tools and play learning games to help expand their knowledge


GibJohn Tutoring was founded in 2024 after feedback from the client and users about gaps in learning and the need for more support to help in achieving goals. Since then, we have been hiring tutors to do face-to-face sessions with our learners to help them improve their knowledge and understanding


As a tutoring company, we hope to hire more tutors with specialist knowledge in some subjects to attract more learners and increase the number of learners who register with us
